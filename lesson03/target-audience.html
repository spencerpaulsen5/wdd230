Target Audience

The Target audience is entrepreneurs that are ready for their business to take off. We especially target those interested in starting business between the ages of 20 and 30.

Persona 1 - Tom

Tom is the owner of a small tax firm. He recently graduated with a bachelors degree in finance, and is currently taking online classes to become a CPA. His main goal is to get his name out there so more people will use his firm. He is trying to stay on top of things with management software, but can find that things can get confusing quickly

Persona 2 - George

George is a 35 year old owner of a car detailing business. He prides himself on being able to get any car looking like new. He believes he needs to change his buisness model so they can take increase profitability.
