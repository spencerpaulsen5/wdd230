Proximity

www.apple.com

Proximity is the distance between things on the page. This is a great example of this, because as you look at the page, the first thing you see is an iphone 14. But they make things fit on the page in such a manner, that you see just the words, of the next product down on their page. In this case, it is the apple watch. Just by doing this, the chance that you are going to scroll down to see the next product increases.

White Space

quip.com

As a society, many times we view the simple and basic as luxurious. Like when you see the ads that have all white furniture in the room. The same goes for this website. They use the white space to make the product feel top of the line.

Repetition

Taylorswift.com

Taylor Swift definetly uses repetion on her website. You can see at the top it gives you a button to pre-order. You can also click any of the albums to pre-order. As you scroll down, there are about 5 more places where you can pre-order. Using repetion, she is able to make it easier for us to pre-order, and for it to be more on our mind as we look at the website
